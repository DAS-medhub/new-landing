Healthcare Challenges

Despite progress in healthcare, many Countries still face significant barriers to quality medical services. Our platform addresses these systemic issues:

  • Delayed diagnosis and treatment due to lack of early symptom assessment
  • Overcrowded hospitals without proper triaging mechanisms
  • Limited access to health professionals in rural areas
  • Fragmented health records across care providers
  • Unsafe self-medication without professional supervision
  • Language and literacy barriers in healthcare
